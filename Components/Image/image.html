Images

Images play an important role in any webpage. Using Ozone library, you can easily make your images responsive.

Responsive Image

Responsive images will automatically adjust to fit the size of the screen. Resize the browser window to see the responsive effect.

To get a responsive image, just add "res-image" class in the img tag.

Round Image

Round Images are the images which are circular in shape.

To get a round shaped image, just add "round-image" class in the img tag.

Square Image

Square Images are the square shaped images.

To get a square shaped image, just add "square-image" class in the img tag.
